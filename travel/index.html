Travel information

By private car

GPS Coordinates: 39.2129501, 9.5635183,17

By public transportation

Public transportation is possible with the bus line n. 101 operated by ARST.
The Sant'Elmo bus stop is not always served, so a taxi might be needed for the last 3/4 km. Public transport is not very efficient, so we recommend our shuttle service.

By our private shuttle transfer

Upon request, shuttle transfers from/to Cagliari Elmas Airport are organized according to flights arrivals/departures. Precise rates will be communicated after all subscriptions will be registered.

Climate & Dressing

October in Sardinia is the start of fall with mild weather of approximately 20-24°C in the daytime and about 14-18 °C in the evenings. We recommend you to bring layered clothing and rain coats for possible showers.

Electricity Supply

The standard electrical voltage in Italy is 220 volts AC, 50Hz with European type connections. (L and F here)

Badges

Wearing badges is mandatory in order to have an access to the scientific sessions, refreshments and social events.

Smoking Policy

The Organization Committee has a no-smoking policy during all scientific sessions. In Italy, smoking is prohibited in all closed areas.
