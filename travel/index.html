Travel information

By private car

GPS Coordinates: 39.926316, 9.711924

By public transportation

From Elmas Airport: take the train to Cagliari’s railway station and follow the point below.

From Piazza Matteotti in Cagliari (ARST Bus Central Station right outside the railway station): take the bus 103 to Tortolì and stop at Tortolì Fra Locci (approx. 3hrs 15 minutes). Then take the bus 305 to Arbatax and stop at Porto Frailis (approx. 15 minutes). The Resort is approx. 15 minutes’ walk from Porto Frailis.

By our private shuttle transfer

From Elmas Airport: shuttle transfers are organized according to flights arrivals/departures. Precise rates will be communicated after all subscriptions will be registered (starting from € 18,00 per person/route in bus 54 pax with full occupation to a maximum of € 78,00 per person/route in private car/minivan with a minimum of 3 passengers. In case the number of passengers is inferior to 3, rates vary from 90,00€ (2 pax) to 160,00€ (1 pax)

Climate & Dressing

September in Sardinia is the ending of summer with mild weather of approximately 21-27 °C in the daytime and about 16-20 °C in the evenings. We recommend you to bring light clothes and rain coats for possible showers.

Electricity Supply

The standard electrical voltage in Italy is 220 volts AC, 50Hz with European type connections. (L and F here)

Badges

Wearing badges is mandatory in order to have an access to the scientific sessions, refreshments and social events.

Smoking Policy

The Organization Committee has a no-smoking policy during all scientific sessions. In Italy, smoking is prohibited in all closed areas.
